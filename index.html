ABOUT THE GAME

Bikol Patuod is an undergraduate research aimed to collect and categorize patuod in Calabanga. This digitalized patuod game is devised in the effort of keeping Bikol Patuod alive, known and preserved.

The researchers, Ma.Christina B. Candelaria and Ferilyn D. Librero, along with the College of Education of Central Bicol State University of Agriculture-Calabanga, designed the Bikol Patuod game as a means of cultural preservation and enrichment of Bikol's verbal arts.

HOW TO PLAY

Choose a Game Mode
Pick how you want to play.

Select a Theme (Optional)
If you choose "By Category," pick a category from Bikol Patuod. If you choose "Normal," just keep going.

Guess the Answer
Fill in the blanks with your guess.

Pass or Shuffle
If a patuod (riddle) is too hard or you can't guess it, click "Pass." If you want to try another patuod, click "Shuffle" and come back to this one later.

Get a Hint
Click the "Hint" button to see jumbled letters that make up the answer.

Guess More Patuod
Keep guessing as many Bikol patuod as you can!
